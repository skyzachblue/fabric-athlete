About Us

Fabric Athlete was founded by a group of first-year university students from Mapúa University, who shared a passion for technology and sports. What began as a simple project for a web development course evolved into a vision to create a brand that offers athletes customized gear designed to fit their unique needs. Our goal is to provide a seamless, user-friendly platform that allows athletes to personalize their products, ensuring both style and performance. From our humble beginnings as students with a bold idea, Fabric Athlete is dedicated to innovating the way athletes shop for gear, making sure that each piece reflects their individual style and enhances their performance.
